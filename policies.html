1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website or place an order:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Payment details (processed securely through third-party gateways)
  • Order history and preferences
  • Device information (IP address, browser type, cookies)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Order history and preferences
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Send promotions, updates, or newsletters (optional)

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share data only with:

  • Payment processors
  • Shipping and logistics partners
  • Order history and preferences
  • Website analytics tools
  • All partners follow strict confidentiality protocols.
